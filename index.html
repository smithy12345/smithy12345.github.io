Synchronized Ice Skating

I started synchronized ice skating when I was 5 years old. Synchronized skating allowed me the opportunity to compete nationally around the United States as well as internationally across Europe. Being a member of TEAM USA is one I will never forget. It was an honor being able to represent my country at the Jr. World Championships in 2009. I continued synchro skating during my college years as well skating for the University of Delaware.
